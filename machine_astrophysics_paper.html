Machine Learning in Astrophysics

A literature review of the role of machine learning in astrophysics research, October 30th, 2018

Introduction

Machine learning is a broad category of algorithms used to perform complex analyses. With an exponentially increasing amount of data needing analysis, automated processing has become more and more important. The astrophysics research field has a rich history of using machine learning techniques in their research, beginning in the 1940s. However, limitations of machine learning technology have prevented many astrophysics researchers from adopting these methods in their studies.
